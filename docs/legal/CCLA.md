## 1. Definitions

**"You" (or "Your")** means the organization that submits Contributions to the Project, and all subsidiaries and affiliates of that organization.

**"Contribution"** means any original work of authorship, including any modifications or additions to an existing work, that is intentionally submitted by any employee, contractor, or agent of Your organization to the Project for inclusion. **"Designated Employees"** means the employees of Your organization who are authorized to submit Contributions. You may update this list by emailing license@hermeticsys.com.

**"Project"** means the Hermetic software project, including all repositories under the `hermetic-sys` GitHub organization.
